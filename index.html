MISSION SPACE

First Indian private Aero2astro research platform's motive is to become an aerospace manufacturer and have a private Rocket launch platform.

MISSION UAV

Aero2astro team is working on a project FLYALOT DRONEAID to bring out a UAV which brings aid to the soldiers at our borders and war zones who are affected by climatic changes, get wounded and lie unconscious.

AVIATION FOR ALL

Team Aero2Astro is on a mission to inspire the youth of our nation to develop
a sense of "air-mindedness", by conducting workshops, events etc., which can lead them into spectacular careers. and most importantly, to teach them the love of flying!

WHAT WE ARE  INNOVATING

The idea of "aviation" should be graspable by all.

For decades the concept of Aviation, Space Travel and Unmanned Aerial Vehicles have been an exclusive sport, not available to all. Our primary mission is to change that, with India leading the global aviation sector, it is time we realize "the next big thing" as how we envision our future.
